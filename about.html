About Bloom & Grow

We are passionate about bringing the beauty of nature into your home. Since 2015, we've been helping plant lovers find the perfect green companions.

Our Story

Bloom & Grow started as a small neighborhood plant shop with a simple mission: to help people connect with nature through plants. What began as a passion project has grown into a beloved destination for plant enthusiasts across the country.

Our founder, Sarah Green, has always believed that plants have the power to transform spaces and lives. With this vision, she set out to create a place where everyone—from seasoned gardeners to first-time plant parents—could find the perfect plant and the knowledge to help it thrive.

Today, we continue to uphold these values while expanding our offerings to include a wide variety of indoor plants, outdoor plants, flowers, and gardening accessories.

Our Values

Sustainability

We source our plants responsibly and use eco-friendly packaging to minimize our environmental impact.

Quality

Every plant is carefully selected and nurtured to ensure it arrives at your door healthy and vibrant.

Community

We believe in building a community of plant lovers who can share knowledge, experiences, and joy.
